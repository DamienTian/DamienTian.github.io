Pyres

To protect your country, sacrifice must me made... 

Job title: Lead Designer, UI designer and developer

This is a Ludum Dare game jam project that I teamed up with one developer and one artist. We created an RTS game base on a fantasy world similar to ancient Europe. The player is the general of the country called Goetilandia. The country was invaded by an unknown enemy at night and 5 cities were under attack. The remaining troops in each city are defending their cities and waiting for your reinforcement. Without your help, all cities will be lost. 

In this project, I created the basic idea and core mechanics. I helped artist create the art style and the theme of the game. I design the UI and developed it in Unity. To corresponding the theme of the game jam "sacrifice must me made", I designed some interesting "traps" in the game to make the players feel how merciless that the war is, and I also designed the general theme of the game to create the bloody feeling of sacrifices in the war.

Feel free to check it on Ludum Dare 43 page: Pyres
