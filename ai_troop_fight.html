AI Troop Fight

AI troops war stimulator 



Job title: Behavior tree developer

This is my final project for CS 5150 Game AI at Northeastern University. In this project, me and Fan Ling developed a war stimulator between two AI troops on Unity. The AI in the game can auto format the formation of the troop in any circumstance. In this project, I developed the behavior tree system for all the AI, the behavior tree can help each AI decides what to do when moving, facing a enemy or fighting. The project receive 95 out of 100 points and was used as one final project example to show to the later semester.

Feel free to check our work on our github page: 5150_FinalProject.
